EXPECTANT PARENTS

Pregnant and considering adoption?

We understand that adoption is one of the many options you have to choose. Our staff is here to support you in making the best decision for you and your baby. We can help you explore and understand the many aspects of adoption, along with your rights as a birth mom.

Here at Parentfinder we have made it easy for you to access the profiles of hundreds of Adoptive Couples and Agency/Attorneys. For your protection and the protection of the Adoptive Couples all communication is run through their Agency/Attorney unless their profile states independent.

My name is Gina and I am a birth mom. I am here to help you. Email me anytime you have questions. You can reach me here: gina.crotts@cairsolutions.com.

Is Parentfinder.com an agency or affiliated with an agency?

No, Parentfinder.com is a safe, secure, and impartial website.

Some families are registered with an agency or attorney. Some families are working independently. That information is posted on each profile under agency info.

Do I have to buy a membership?
There is no fee for an expectant parent to join Parentfinder.com. If you are interested in becoming a member, simply click on the join button and follow the instructions to complete your membership.

Will my personal information remain private and confidential?

Always, if you are searching as a guest and you contact a family via the click to call service your personal information will be anonymous. If you register as a member and contact a family they will be able to view whatever you post on your profile.

BIRTH MOTHER BASKETS
Birth Mother Baskets {bmb} is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that delivers gift baskets to birth mothers who have recently placed their babies for adoption. Our baskets contain unique, high quality, handpicked items that are made to pamper a birth mom. bmb is a gift from one birth mother to another that offers hope, support and courage.
