THE PROCESS
Once the homestudy is approved, the selected agency and family contacts us, and an initial meeting is scheduled via Skype or Go To Meeting.

The purpose of this meeting is for us to get to know and understand the family on a deeper level. We are interested in, and will discuss, what kind of parents they want to be; their values; personality; budget; home life; hobbies; career paths; and other important information.
Once we gather the preliminary information from the chosen adoption agency, three meetings are scheduled with the family to help educate and coach them on the adoption process; creating a customized profile; launching and marketing their profiles; and how to review and make adjustments to their profile based on internet traffic data.


Meetings to measure results will continue each month until a match is made with an e-mom or birthmother.
